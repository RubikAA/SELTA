About SELTA:

SELTA( SELector of Teacher Assistants), is a web based app, helping professors choose their assistants. We are a team of about 10 university studentds, from Sharif university of technology, working on this project.

SELTA is not an economical project, but an educational one, and if it could be successful, it would be probably released and used by our university.
